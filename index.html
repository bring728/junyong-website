Research

My main research field are 3D computer vision and deep learning. I am interested in light-related research such as lighting estimation, inverse rendering, and view synthesis.

AAAI, 2024
project page / arXiv

MAIR: Multi-view Attention Inverse Rendering with 3D Spatially-Varying Lighting Estimation
JunYong Choi, SeokYeong Lee, Haesol Park, Seung-Won Jung, Ig-Jae Kim, Junghyun Cho
CVPR, 2023
project page / arXiv

We propose a scene-level inverse rendering framework that uses multi-view images to decompose the scene into geometry, a SVBRDF, and 3D spatially-varying lighting.
